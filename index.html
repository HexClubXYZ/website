How to shorten your ETH address
1. Get the first 5 characters of your ETH wallet eg: 0x4b8
2. Go to https://app.ens.domains
3. Register your shortened address: 0x4b8.eth
4. If your first 5 characters are taken, use 6 characters.
5. Welcome to the HexClub.
